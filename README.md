1. Title

Interactive Sales Forecasting & Performance Dashboard

2. Short Description

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze historical sales data, track performance across markets, and forecast future trends to support better business decision-making.

3. Purpose

The main goal of this project is to help businesses understand their sales performance and predict future demand. It enables stakeholders to identify trends, optimize inventory, and make data-driven decisions quickly using visual insights.

4. Tech Stack

Power BI – Data visualization and dashboard creation

Databricks / Python – Data processing and forecasting

Excel / CSV – Data source

DAX – Calculations and measures

5. Data Source

The dataset consists of sales transactions including date (monthly sales data), market or city (Delhi, Mumbai, etc.), revenue, sales quantity, customers, and products. This structured data helps in analyzing trends and generating meaningful insights.

6. Features and Highlights

Revenue trend analysis for monthly performance tracking

Market-wise sales insights to identify top-performing regions

Top 5 products identification based on revenue

Top customers analysis for business prioritization

Dynamic filters for year and month selection

Clean and interactive dashboard design for better usability

7. Business Impact and Insights

The dashboard reveals that Delhi is the highest revenue-generating market. A noticeable decline in revenue after March highlights potential seasonal or external impacts, enabling early intervention. The analysis of top customers shows that a small group contributes significantly to overall revenue, helping businesses focus on key accounts. Additionally, the insights support better inventory planning and demand forecasting, ultimately improving strategic decision-making.
